L
  • He led farmers in various successful Satyagraha in Kheda 1918,Borsad 1924 and Bardoli 1928
  • Elected as Ahmedabad's Municipality President in 1924 and worked till April 1928
  • Elected President of the Indian National Congress in 1931 in Karachi Session
  • Independent India's first Deputy Prime Minister,Homeminister and Information and Broadcasting Minister
  • The Architech of United India Post-Independence
  • Held the charge of Prime Minister of India four times on different occasions till 1950
  • Charted the Indian Constitution by drafting provision for important subjects as a Chairman
  • Posthumously, conferred with Bharat Ratna in 1991
